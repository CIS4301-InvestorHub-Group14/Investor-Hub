Here are the applicable metrics!

Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD)

A trend-following momentum indicator that shows the relationship between two exponential moving averages (EMAs) of a security's price.

Volatility

A measure of dispersion around the mean return of a security.

Relative Strength Index (RSI)

A technical momentum indicator that compares recent price gains against recent price losses indicating whether or not an asset is overbought or oversold.

Monthly Moving Average (MMA)

A technical indicator that averages a stock's price over a specified period of one month, smoothing out short-term fluctuations and highlighting longer-term trends in price movement.

Positive and Negative Volume Index

The Positive Volume Index (PVI) tracks upward price movement on days of increased volume, while the Negative Volume Index (NVI) tracks price movement on days of decreased volume, both aiming to identify trends based on trading volume changes.
